from sklearn.metrics import accuracy_score, roc_auc_score, roc_curve,auc
def c_statistics(model, x_test):
    y_pred = model.predict_proba(x_test)
    fpr , tpr , thresholds = roc_curve(y_test, y_pred[:,1])
    plt.plot(fpr, tpr, color = 'darkorange')
    plt.title('ROC Curve (area = %0.2f)' % auc(fpr, tpr))
    #plt.plot([0,1], [0,1], linestyles-'--')
    plt.plot(fpr, tpr)
    plt.xlabel('False Positive Rate')
    plt.ylabel('True Positive Rate')
    plt.xlim(0,)
    plt.ylim(0,)
    plt.show()
    plt.close()

Dataset analysis and recommendations¶

EVENT1_30_FLAG attribute is not contributing towards making a decision that customer will buy the policy through phone channel "CHANNEL11_6M" is highly correlated with LATEPAYMENT_6M. Thus we consider one attribute. LATE_PAYMENT_3M, LATE_PAYMENT_6M, and "CHANNEL1_6M" are highly correlated. Thus we will select one of the features. Tenure and Age attributes are highly correlated "CHANNLE1_6M". Age and Tenure attributes are highly correlated with MEHOD1_3M. Age and Tenure attributes are higly correlated with lATE_PAYEMT_6M.
